Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-09

This Privacy Policy describes how the Atlas Pulse Auto Poster application ("the Application") handles data when it accesses third-party APIs on behalf of its sole operator. The Application is operated solely by the owner of the @atlaspulsetv channels.

1. Information We Access

The Application connects to the following platform using OAuth 2.0 authorization granted by the operator's own account:

  • TikTok Content Posting API (via the operator's TikTok account @atlaspulsetv)

The Application only accesses the operator's own account credentials. It does not access, collect, store, or process information about any other TikTok user.

2. How We Use Information

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are used solely to upload video content to the operator's own channels on a scheduled basis. The Application does not:

  • Read content from other users
  • Send messages, comments, or follows to other users
  • Share information with third parties
  • Use information for advertising or analytics across services

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer in encrypted form and are never transmitted to any external server other than the official TikTok API endpoints. Video files and metadata are stored locally and uploaded directly to TikTok.

4. Data Retention

Tokens are retained only as long as needed to perform scheduled uploads. The operator may revoke access at any time through the TikTok account settings.

5. Third-Party Services

This Application uses the official API provided by TikTok. The TikTok privacy policy applies to data processed by that service:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

This Application is operated by an adult for an educational content channel. It is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: atlaspulsetv@gmail.com
